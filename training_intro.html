Modules

The content is divined into 3 modules. Each module is divided on lessons, wchich consist of: slides, exercises, and readings.

  • Module 1 - A journey from high level language, through assembly, to the running process
  • Module 2 - Typical goals of malware and their implementations
  • Module 3 - Understanding and countering malware evasion and self-defence
